PaperFaces Project

Gallery of portraits drawn with Paper by FiftyThree.

PaperFaces was an illustration project by designer Michael Rose — hey that’s me! For two years I drew the faces of strangers everyday using an iPad, a stylus, and Paper by FiftyThree. I occasionally post new portraits here, but certainly not as frequently as I used to.

If you scroll down far enough you can see how my technique evolved from faceless gesture into realistic portraits.
